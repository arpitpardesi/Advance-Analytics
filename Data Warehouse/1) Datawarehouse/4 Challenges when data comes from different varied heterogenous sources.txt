Challenges when data comes from different varied heterogenous sources:
1) Data types may be different
2) Size may be different even if data type is same
3) Date Formats may be different
4) ** Business Rules or Data Validations rules are different
5) Nullability rules can be different

1) Full Load -- First time load

2) Incremental Load -- Every day load, generally this will be Scheduled
