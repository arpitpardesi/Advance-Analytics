Training Protocol:
1) By default all will be in mute mode.
2) Rules of Interaction of student:
    In Chat window type the following:
        y for yes
        n for no
        c for clear
        q for question or query or doubt
        qs? for can I speak and ask question
        r for repeat
        b for came back
        a for audible
        na for not audible
        ci for connectivity issues
